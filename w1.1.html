Safety & Tools

Safely Issues

Fablab @ Singapore Polytechnic has many tools, equipment and machinery which can be used to fabricate projects. In the course of the module we will be taught how to operate these tools and equipment but most importantly, lab safety.

The Safety Assessment is compulsory for all users of Fablab@SP and materials for it can be found in blackboard. However due to the current COVID19 situation, the assessment will be done at a later date when we can go back to the lab.
